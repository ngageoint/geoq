Jobs

Jobs are geospatial work that can be assigned over large geographic areas. This work is further divided into manageable tasks called "AOIs".

Areas of Interest

Areas of Interest are a collection of manageable geospatial tasks in a well-defined location. AOIs are the smallest unit of organization in GeoQ, each of which is assigned to a single user to complete.
